How To Play Salsa!

Setup Your Account

Setting up your account is easy! Just follow these steps:

  1. Put your address in here
  2. Receive your token
  3. Send the token back to us, to the address given to you after you submit your address
  4. Wait to be verified
  5. Start your ventures as a miner!

How to play

The game is fairly simple:

  1. You get sent some SALSA every day, based on how much salsa you already have and how many pickaxes you have.
  2. You can buy pickaxes for 10 RVN each, for every pickaxe you have your get 100% more salsa!
  3. Sell your hard-earned SALSA to the highest bidder on RavenX!
Pro-tip: use your dashboard to keep track of all your SALSAing!
